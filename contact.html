Frequently Asked Questions

Is NexaCon 2026 in-person only? +
Yes — NexaCon 2026 is a fully in-person event at BIEC, Bangalore. We believe the best connections happen face-to-face. Selected keynotes may be streamed online.
Can students attend? +
Absolutely. Students are a core part of the NexaCon community. Early bird passes are available at the standard price — apply for a student discount via email if needed.
How do I participate in the hackathon? +
All registered attendees can participate. Teams of 2–4 are formed on Day 2. Solo registrations are welcome — we'll help you find a team onsite.
Is accommodation included? +
Accommodation is not included in the ticket price. We've partnered with nearby hotels for discounted rates — details will be emailed after registration.
Can I get a refund? +
Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person free of charge.
